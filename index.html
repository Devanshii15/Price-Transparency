Our health care price transparency solution

COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS-


A comprehensive “machine-readable” file that includes the standard charges for all hospital items and services. A consumer-friendly display that includes the standard charges for at least 300 ‘shoppable’ services (or as many as the hospital provides if less than 300) that are grouped with any of the ancillary service charges also provided by the hospital.
New Future Rules:

Creates a price transparency requirement for insurance companies to share their negotiated rates with their patients and public- effective January 1, 2021

PENALTIES FOR NON-COMPLIANCE-


• If a hospital is believed to be noncompliant with the above requirements to make standard charges public, CMS may issue a warning notice or request a corrective action plan.

• If the hospital does not submit or comply with the corrective action plan, CMS may then assess a monetary penalty, if the hospital is in direct violation of the requirements. Monetary penalties- max $300 per day.

• Publish hospital name on CMS website, for noncompliance.
